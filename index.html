Ghost Writer

Write in plain text or markdown, then use Render Output to turn your draft into navigable HTML. After you leave the editor and move through the page, the ghost may start tampering with what you wrote.

Draft your text

Use the editor to write notes, lists, links, and headings. The ghost only interferes after you render your work or leave the editor.

Rendered output

This area renders your draft as real HTML that can be navigated with a keyboard or screen reader.

Markdown basics

Ghost Writer accepts plain text, but these common markdown patterns can help you structure your notes.

  • # Heading 1
  • ## Heading 2
  • - List item
  • 1. Numbered item
  • [Link text](https://example.com)
  • **bold** and *italic*
  • `inline code`
